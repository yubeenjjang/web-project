TRAVEL

Malaysia

I visited Malaysia in 2010, 2012, and 2014. My aunt lived there. It was hard because it was hotter than Korea when I first visited. But it was nice to have more variety of tropical fruits. I went with my grandmother and grandfather in 2010 and with my brother in 2012. In 2014, I flew by myself. Malaysia airport was so big that I had to take the subway when I got off the plane, but I was worried that I might get lost, but I met my aunt safely. It was probably the biggest challenge of my life.

Japan

I traveled to Japan with my mom for 2 nights and 3 days in May 2018. I went to Universal Studios and my mom had a hard time riding the scary roller coaster because of me. And Japanese convenience store food was delicious. The pudding was especially delicious. I want to go with my friends again later.

Jeju Island

I have been to Jeju island many times, but the most recent time I went was in the second grade of high school. It was more fun because I went with my friends. It was fun to see the sea and Dolharubang, but it was more memorable to watch a movie after ordering chicken in the evening. And I went to Jeju Island in the fall, and I think it would be better to go in the summer.

Busan

I went on a trip with my friends for the first time as an adult. And it is my first visit to Busan. I took a plane for the first time in a long time, so I went around a lot of Busan restaurants and enjoyed the sea. Curiously, the sea didn't smell fishy. So it was better and more fun because I was with my friends.

I want to travel all the time
